Westlife - The Wild Dreams Tour

Where and When

Fri, July 8 2022

8:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Aviva Stadium

Lansdowne Road

About this event

2 hours

Mobile eTicket

The Irish pop vocal group Westlife is now on a concert tour called the Wild Dreams Tour, which was formerly known as the Stadiums in the Summer Tour. The original plan was for 22 performances across Europe, starting on June 17, 2020, at Scarborough Open Air Theatre in England, and ending on August 29, 2020, at Páirc Ui Chaoimh in Cork, Ireland.

The COVID-19 pandemic finally forced the postponement of the tour. The band has moved dates to 2022, including ones at Wembley Stadium, Cork, and Scarborough. Only two of the initially stated 17 additional tour dates have been postponed. They were the first international group to perform three times in a single tour at Singapore Indoor Stadium thanks to their three performances there.
